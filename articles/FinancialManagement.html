9. PPF (Public Provident Fund

Key points :

  • 15 years period scheme
  • 100% Government security scheme
  • You get up to 8.7% interest rate
  • Here, we can save from Rs 500 up to Rs 1,50,000
  • Joint account is possible between parents and their children
  • You have to invest only 12 times in a given financial year.
  • In a 15-year investment scheme, you have an option of withdrawing 50% of your total investment after 7 years or you can chose to withdraw after the term is over.
  • Parents might want to take initiative and make investments for 15 years to secure their kids financially in future.
